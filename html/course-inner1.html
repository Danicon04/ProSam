Course Overview

Welcome to Accelerated Piano Training 2.0, a carefully structured online learning experience designed specifically for novices and absolute beginners who are eager to master the piano from the ground up. Whether your'e picking up the instrument for the first time or looking to strengthen your foundational skills, this course offers a concise, effective, and engaging path toward piano proficiency.

At ProSam Music, we understand the challenges that come with learning a musical instrument, especially without prior knowledge. That's why this course is built on simplicity, practicality, and consistent progress. With a unique blend of instructional videos, a free eBook, and interactive live sessions, you'll gain hands-on training that fits seamlessly into your schedule and learning pace.

What you'll learn

Understand the basic layout of the piano keyboard and hand positioning.

Learn how to read basic sheet music and chord notations.

Master essential scales and fingering techniques.

Play popular songs using both hands with increasing fluency.

Develop your ear-training skills and rhythmic accuracy.

Gain confidence to play in solo or group settings.
