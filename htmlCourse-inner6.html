Course Overview

"Piano Basics by Ear" is a comprehensive guidebook that teaches piano playing without relying on sheet music. It's aimed at both novices and beginners who want to take their skills to the next level by playing by ear, listening to music and experimenting with different notes and chords. The book includes a few exercises and tips to help pianists of any skill level master the basics of piano playing, with easy-to-follow instructions and helpful diagrams. It's a fun and creative way to explore music for everyone.

What you'll learn

Understand intervals & it's significance.

Play Basic Chords and Progressions.

Develop your ear-training skills and rhythmic accuracy.

Use Major, Minor, 7th Chords & Basic Non-Diatonic chords in song progressions.
