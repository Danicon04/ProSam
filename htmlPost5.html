WHAT'S THE DIFFERENCE BETWEEN PIANO, KEYBOARD & ORGAN

This question have been dealt with over and over, it's like we don't read... A piano is a unique keyboard instrument with only the piano sound made by hammers hitting strings within the piano housing when the keys are being played ... The keyboard on the other hand is an electronic musical instruments model after the piano but can produce several other sounds of instruments, nature and mod synths. The keyboard is played by keys also similar to the piano but the sounds are electronically processed... The organ is a keyboard instrument of one or more pipe divisions or other means for producing tones, each played from its own manually, with the hands, or pedalboard, with the feet.
